# Shinobi World

**Shinobi World** is a 2D open-world shinobi RPG about growing from an unknown academy student into a legendary ninja, Kage, feared rogue ninja, master of forbidden techniques, or something stranger entirely.

The goal is to create a world where every run can feel different: a different starting village, a different natural chakra affinity, different jutsus to discover, different rivals to survive, and different choices that shape the path of your shinobi life.

## The Fantasy

You begin as a young shinobi with limited skill, a random origin, and a chakra nature that defines what kind of techniques come naturally to you. From there, the world opens up.

You can train, explore, fight, discover jutsus, build your identity, earn rank, abandon your village, become hunted, form contracts with summons, and learn how far your talent can really go.

Shinobi World is built around the feeling that the player is not just selecting attacks from a list. You are learning a martial art, mastering hand seals, experimenting with chakra, and surviving a living ninja world.

## Current Experience

The current demo focuses on the first playable foundation:

- Create a new shinobi.
- Start in a random village.
- Receive a natural chakra nature and secondary affinity.
- Explore an overworld map.
- Encounter enemies in the world.
- Enter turn-based combat when conflict starts.
- Use basic attacks and jutsus.
- Practice techniques outside combat.
- Watch the world begin to fill with villages, ninjas, and future story possibilities.

The visuals and content are still early, but the core identity is already in place: exploration, progression, chakra, jutsu, enemies, and the first version of turn-based shinobi combat.

## What The Game Will Become

Shinobi World is planned as a long-form open-world RPG with many possible lives:

- Rise through the ranks from Academy Student to Genin, Chuunin, Jounin, Anbu, Sanin and Kage.
- Choose loyalty to a village or walk the rogue ninja path.
- Discover jutsus by training, experimenting, and learning hand seal sequences. Even create your own jutsus
- Improve jutsus over time so they cost less chakra, hit harder, or require fewer seals.
- Fight enemies whose speed, tactics, chakra, and jutsu knowledge matter.
- Survive lethal attacks through reaction windows that can let skilled players evade or counter.
- Explore during day and night cycles.
- Use jutsus in the overworld, not only in combat.
- Discover summon contracts with creatures such as frogs, snakes, hawks, and dogs.
- Seal objects, tools, or even people into scrolls.
- Summon stored weapons or items when needed.
- Use clones and utility techniques for exploration.
- Watch villages, ninjas, rogues, and threats grow into a larger world.

## Core Ideas

### A Different Shinobi Every Run

Each new character should feel like a real start to a new life. Your village, chakra nature, secondary affinity, ability rolls, and early jutsu options create a different foundation every time.

### Jutsus Are Learned, Not Just Equipped

Jutsus are meant to feel like techniques you understand. Hand seals matter. Chakra nature matters. Skill matters. A player who experiments and trains should be able to uncover new possibilities instead of simply buying every ability from a menu.

### Combat Is Turn-Based, But Dangerous

Battles are planned around speed, chakra, timing, and risk. Strong attacks can become deadly moments where a reaction window gives the defender one last chance to evade, counter, or survive.

### The World Is Bigger Than The Player

Villages, enemies, rogues, summons, rankings, and reputations are intended to exist beyond a single scripted route. The long-term goal is a world that can support many stories instead of one fixed path.

### Player Skill Should Matter

The game is not only about stats. Knowing jutsu sequences, managing chakra, reacting under pressure, choosing when to fight, and deciding when to flee should all matter.

## Why It Stands Out

Shinobi World aims to combine the structure of a classic RPG with the fantasy of becoming a true shinobi:

- Open-world exploration instead of a simple battle menu.
- Discoverable techniques instead of only predefined unlock lists.
- Hand seal mastery as part of progression.
- Combat where speed, chakra, and reaction matter.
- Multiple life paths, including village loyalty and rogue survival.
- A growing world designed for many villages, enemies, jutsus, summons, and stories.

## Development Status

Shinobi World is in early development. The first demo is focused on proving the main loop:

**create a shinobi, enter the world, explore, encounter enemies, fight, use jutsus, and continue growing.**

Many systems are still simple, but the direction is clear: a scalable shinobi life simulator where exploration, combat, training, experimentation, and world progression all connect.

## Long-Term Vision

The finished game should feel like starting a new shinobi life inside a dangerous world that does not revolve entirely around you.

You may become a loyal protector, a feared rogue, a wandering master, a summon-bonded specialist, a forbidden-jutsu researcher, or a survivor who simply learned when to run.

The dream is simple:

**Every shinobi should have a story. Every jutsu should feel earned. Every fight should have weight.**
